# 🌸 Hidden Workload Tracker

A simple web-based tool designed to identify and manage hidden workload, especially for working women balancing both professional and household responsibilities.

---

## 🧠 Problem

Many working women handle multiple responsibilities throughout the day.  
However, a large part of their work is not clearly tracked, and priorities keep changing.

Because of this:
- Workload keeps increasing without clear visibility  
- Tasks feel overwhelming  
- Effort often goes unnoticed  
- This leads to stress and mental fatigue  

---

## 💡 Approach

Instead of building a complex solution, the focus was on a small but practical improvement:

👉 Making hidden workload **visible and structured**

The initial idea was a simple daily task list.  
This was then improved into a structured system that reflects real-life usage.

---

## ⚙️ Features (with purpose)

### 📌 Task Separation
- Daily Routine (Regular Tasks)  
- Today's Additional Tasks  

👉 Helps users understand how much work is routine vs extra.

---

### 🎯 Priority Levels
- High / Medium / Low  

👉 Helps focus on important tasks instead of feeling overwhelmed.

---

### 📊 Workload Breakdown
- Total tasks  
- Regular vs Today's tasks  

👉 Gives a clear picture of workload instead of a vague feeling of “too much work”.

---

### 💬 Insight Message

Shows simple feedback like:
- Workload is balanced  
- Daily tasks are taking more effort  
- Extra tasks are increasing workload  

👉 Helps users quickly understand their situation.

---

### 💖 Emotional Feedback

In real life, even after completing a lot of work, there is often no appreciation.  
This can make users feel demotivated.

To address this, the system provides small motivational feedback.

✨ Example:  
**“🎉 Great job! You completed an important task!”**

👉 This acts as a subtle voice of appreciation, helping users feel recognized and encouraged.

---

### ✏️ Edit Tasks

👉 Allows flexibility as tasks change during the day.
